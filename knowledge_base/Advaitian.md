# ADVAITIAN PHILOSOPHY OF PROBLEM SOLVING

## Core Vision
Mathematics is not a collection of isolated techniques but a finite landscape of elegant structures. Mastery comes from internalizing ~20 fundamental patterns (archetypes) and learning to orchestrate them multidimensionally.

## The Three Pillars
1. **The Seed:** The underlying meta-pattern (Archetype).
2. **The Brute Path:** The mechanical, inefficient approach a student naturally tries.
3. **The Elegant Pivot:** The key insight that makes the solution trivial.

## The Six-Point Framework
Every commentary must include:
1. SEED
2. BRUTE PATH
3. ELEGANT PIVOT
4. PITFALLS
5. CONNECTIONS
6. TAKEAWAY

## The Two-Stage Process
1. **Stage 1 (MVC):** Conceptual validation of the seed and pivot.
2. **Stage 2 (Formalization):** Detailed expansion into the full framework.

*This philosophy serves as the foundation for the Advaitian Commentary Engine.*
